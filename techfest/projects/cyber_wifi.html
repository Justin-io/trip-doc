Project: WiFi Phishing (Evil Twin)
Zone: Cyber-Gym (Green) | ID: P-12
1. Overview
"Spot the Trap". Participants connect to a WiFi network. They must identify which one is legitimate and which is the "Evil Twin" designed to steal credentials.
2. Tech Stack
NodeMCU (ESP8266 Deauther) or Kali Linux (Airgeddon).
