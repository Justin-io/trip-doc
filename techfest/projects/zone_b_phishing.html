Project Details: The Phishing Tank
Zone: B (Cyber-Lab) ID: B-01
1. Overview
A cybersecurity awareness challenge where participants identify fake emails, cloned websites, and malicious WiFi access points.
2. Challenges
  • Email: Users spot the typo-squatted domain (e.g., support@gogle.com).
  • Login Page: Differentiating a cloned Facebook login from the real one.
  • WiFi: Identifying the "Evil Twin" access point.
3. Rewards
  • 3/3 Correct: 20 FestCoins
