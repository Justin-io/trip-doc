Project Details: Deadshot.io Tournament
Zone: A (The Arena) ID: A-01
1. Overview
A high-octane 1v1 FPS tournament hosted in the browser using Deadshot.io. This serves as the primary crowd-puller for the event zone.
2. Gameplay & Rules
  • Format: 1v1 Deathmatch (3 Minute Rounds).
  • Rewards:
    • Winner: 50 FestCoins
    • Loser: 10 FestCoins
    • Bonus Challenge: Defeat "Sentinel AI" Bot for 100 FestCoins.
3. Technical Stack
  • Platform: Web Browser (Chrome/Brave).
  • Network: Low-latency LAN/WLAN.
  • Hardware: 2x Gaming PCs + 1x Spectator Screen.
