Infrastructure: The Coin System
Zone: Infrastructure (Yellow) | ID: P-22
1. Overview
The central digital economy of TechFest. Tracks user points (FestCoins) via QR codes.
2. Workflow
QR Check-in -> Play Game -> Scan Winner QR at Station -> Balance Update -> Redeem at Potion Shop.
3. Tech Stack
Firebase (Realtime DB), React PWA, QR Scanner Lib.
