Project: Invisible Cloak

Zone A: The Arena

1. Overview

A "magic" experience inspired by Harry Potter. When a user holds a specific colored cloth (e.g., Red or Green), the camera renders the background instead of the cloth, making them appear transparent.

2. User Experience

  • Users stand in front of a fixed camera.
  • They wrap themselves in the "Cloak".
  • On the screen, their body disappears, showing only the background behind them.
  • Reward: 10 FestCoins (Experience only).

3. Technical Implementation

  • Stack: Python (OpenCV).
  • Algorithm:
    1. Capture reference background frame (2-3 seconds).
    2. Detect specific color (HSV Range for Red/Blue) to create a mask.
    3. Invert Mask: Segment out the user.
    4. Combine: Replace masked pixels with reference background pixels.

4. Requirements

  • 1 Tripod (Camera MUST be stationary).
  • Solid monochrome cloth (Bright Red or Green).
  • Lighting control (avoid shadows).
