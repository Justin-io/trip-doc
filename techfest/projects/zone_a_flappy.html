Project: Nose-Nav Flappy Bird

Zone A: The Arena

1. Overview

An interactive twist on the classic Flappy Bird game. Instead of tapping a screen, players control the bird's movement using their nose position, tracked via a webcam.

2. Gameplay & Rules

  • Control: Move head UP to fly higher, DOWN to dive.
  • Objective: Pass 10 pipes to win.
  • Coin Reward:
    • Score > 10: 40 FestCoins
    • Score < 10: 5 FestCoins

3. Technical Implementation

  • Stack: Python (PyGame) or JavaScript (p5.js).
  • Vision Library:
    • MediaPipe Face Mesh: Tracks the nose tip landmark (Index 4).
    • Logic: rapid changes in Y-coordinate trigger the "jump" event.

4. Requirements

  • 1 PC/Laptop with a good Webcam.
  • Large Monitor for the player.
  • Good lighting for face detection.
