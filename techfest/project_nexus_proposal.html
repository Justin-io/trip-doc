EVENT PROPOSAL: PROJECT NEXUS
(TechFest 2026 - Organizing Committee)
Theme: Gamifying the Future Date: 03-02-2026
1. Executive Summary
"Project Nexus" is a proposal to transform the standard technical exhibition into a fully interactive Cyber-Arcade. By gamifying 27 distinct projects into a unified "Event Game," we enable participants to "Play-to-Earn" across competitive gaming, AI demos, and cybersecurity challenges. Unlike traditional stalls where students passively listen, "Nexus" ensures every attendee is an active player in a digital economy.
2. The Core Mechanic: "FestCoin" Economy
A centralized economy drives engagement. Every interaction is tracked digitally, incentivizing students to visit more stalls to earn rewards.
