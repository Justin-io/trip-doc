EVENT PROPOSAL: PROJECT NEXUS
(TechFest 2026 - Organizing Committee)
Theme: Gamifying the Future Date: 03-02-2026
1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
"Project Nexus" is a proposal to transform the standard technical exhibition into a fully interactive Cyber-Arcade. By gamifying 27 distinct projects into a unified "Event Game," we enable participants to "Play-to-Earn" across competitive gaming, AI demos, and cybersecurity challenges.
2. THE CORE MECHANIC: "FESTCOIN"
A centralized economy drives engagement:
  • Entry (The UID): Every student receives a QR-Code Badge.
  • The Loop: Check-in -> Play Games -> Earn Coins -> Redeem Prizes.
  • Utility: Coins can be spent at the IoT Water Dispensary (The Potion Shop) or for physical rewards.
