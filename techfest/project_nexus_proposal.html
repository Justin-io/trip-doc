1. Executive Summary

"Project Nexus" is not just a technical exhibition; it is a fully interactive Cyber-Arcade. Unlike traditional fests where students passively look at screens, this event turns every attendee into a "Player."

Using a centralized Coin System, students will engage with 27 distinct projects across Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and IoT. By participating in games and challenges, they earn digital currency ("FestCoins") to redeem for rewards, ensuring maximum engagement and footfall.

2. The Core Mechanic: "FestCoin"

To drive participation, the entire event is gamified using a custom-built loop:

  • 01. The UID: Upon entry, every student receives a digital/physical badge with a unique QR Code.
  • 02. The Grind: Students visit project stalls and PLAY to EARN.
    • Win a match in Deadshot.io → +50 Coins
    • Beat the AI in Flappy Bird → +40 Coins
    • Pass the Password Strength Test → +25 Coins
  • 03. The Scan: Volunteers scan QR codes after wins, instantly updating the central database.
  • 04. The Leaderboard: Live projector display of the "Top 10 Players".
  • 05. Redemption: Spend coins at the IoT Smart Dispenser or Prize Counter.
