SESSION CURRICULUM: DATA SCIENCE & AI 2026
Duration: 15 Minutes (Flash Talk) | Level: Intermediate
1. SESSION OBJECTIVE
To explain the pipeline of "Data to Decision" and demystify Generative AI. Students should understand that AI is not magic, but math (Probability & Statistics) applied at scale.
2. AGENDA BREAKDOWN
Time Topic & Key Talking Points
0:00 - 4:00 The Data Pipeline
- Collection -> Cleaning -> EDA -> Modeling -> Deployment.
- "Garbage In, Garbage Out" principle.
- Example: How Netflix recommends movies.
4:00 - 8:00 Neural Networks 101
- Biological Neuron vs Artificial Neuron.
- Layers (Input, Hidden, Output).
- Visual Demo: Reference the "Real Model NN" project in Zone B.
8:00 - 12:00 The Generative AI Boom
- Discriminative AI (Classifying functionality) vs Generative AI (Creating new data).
- How LLMs predict the "Next Word".
- How Diffusion Models remove noise to create images (AI Artist Zone C).
12:00 - 15:00 Ethics & Careers
- Bias in Data.
- Career: Data Analyst vs Data Engineer vs ML Scientist.
3. KEY CONCEPTS TO COVER
  • Supervised vs Unsupervised Learning: Labeled vs Unlabeled data.
  • Overfitting: Memorizing data vs Learning patterns.
  • Prompt Engineering: The new skill set for 2026.
4. STUDENT RESOURCES
  • Libraries: Pandas, Scikit-Learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch.
  • Datasets: Kaggle, UC Irvine Repo.
  • Tools: Jupyter Notebooks, Google Colab.
